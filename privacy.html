Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

At Javed Nihari, your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you order from our website, how we use it, and how we keep it safe. By using our website or placing an order, you agree to the practices described below.

What information we collect

When you place an order on our website, we collect the following information:

  • Your name — so our team and rider know who the order is for
  • Your phone number — to contact you for order confirmation or delivery coordination
  • Your delivery address and area — for home delivery orders only
  • Your order details — items, quantities, and any special instructions
  • Pickup branch choice — if you select pickup instead of delivery

We do not collect government ID numbers, credit card information, or your email address.

How we use your information

Your information is used only to fulfill your order. Specifically:

  • To prepare and deliver your food to the correct address
  • To call you if we need to confirm details or there is a delivery issue
  • To send your order summary to our kitchen via WhatsApp
  • To keep a record of your order in case of complaints or refunds

We do not sell your information to anyone. We do not send marketing messages without your permission.

How long we keep your information

Order records are kept for 6 months for customer service and accounting purposes. After 6 months, your personal details are deleted from our system.

If you want your information deleted earlier, contact us at the WhatsApp number below and we'll remove it within 7 working days.

How we protect your information

Your order information is stored securely on Google Firebase, an industry-standard cloud database. Access to your information is limited to authorized Javed Nihari staff only.

Our website uses HTTPS encryption to protect data while it's being sent.

Payment information

We currently accept cash on delivery only. We do not process or store credit card or online payment information on our website. You pay our rider directly when your food arrives.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies — only to remember items in your shopping cart during your visit. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

Children

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If a parent believes their child has provided information to us, please contact us so we can remove it.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to run our website:

  • Google Firebase — to store your order securely
  • WhatsApp — to send your order to our kitchen
  • Google Maps — to show our branch locations

Each of these services has its own privacy policy.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what information we have about you
  • Request a copy of your order history
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Correct any wrong information

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via WhatsApp.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be noted at the top of this page with a new "Last updated" date. Continuing to use our website after changes means you accept the updated policy.
