Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering from Javed Nihari.

How long will my delivery take?

+

Most orders are delivered within 40–60 minutes from confirmation. Actual time depends on your area, traffic, and current order volume. During peak hours (lunch and dinner) it may take a little longer.

What areas do you deliver to?

+

We deliver across most of Karachi, including FB Area, Gulshan-e-Iqbal, Nazimabad, Bahadurabad, PECHS, Tariq Road, Clifton, DHA, Gulistan-e-Johar, Malir, and more.

Delivery fees vary by distance from our branches (Rs. 150 – Rs. 400). The exact fee for your area is shown at checkout when you select it.

If your area is not in our list, please WhatsApp us at 0333-3672590 to check.

How much are delivery charges?

+

Delivery charges depend on your area:

• Rs. 150 — FB Area, Gulberg, Nazimabad
• Rs. 200 — Gulshan-e-Iqbal, North Nazimabad, Liaquatabad
• Rs. 250 — Bahadurabad, PECHS, Tariq Road, Garden
• Rs. 300 — Clifton, DHA Phase 1-2, Saddar
• Rs. 350 — DHA Phase 3-8, Defence View, Korangi
• Rs. 400 — Malir, Gulistan-e-Johar, Scheme 33

You can also choose pickup at no extra charge from either of our branches.

Is there a minimum order amount?

+

Yes — the minimum order for delivery is Rs. 500. There is no minimum for pickup orders.

What are your opening hours?

+

We are open daily from 11:00 AM to 12:00 AM (midnight).

Online ordering follows the same hours. If you visit our website outside these hours, you will see a "We're currently closed" notice — orders can be placed once we open the next day.

How do I pay for my order?

+

We currently accept Cash on Delivery (COD) only. Pay our rider directly when your food arrives.

For pickup orders, you pay at the counter when you collect your order.

What is your refund and exchange policy?

+

Food that has already been consumed cannot be refunded or exchanged.

If there is a genuine complaint about your order (wrong item, food quality, missing items), please contact us within 30 minutes of delivery. Our rider will pick up the food and we will provide a replacement or refund based on the situation.

To report an issue, WhatsApp us at 0333-3672590 with your order number.

How do I submit a complaint or feedback?

+

We take all feedback seriously. You can reach us by:

• WhatsApp: 0333-3672590 (fastest)
• Phone: 0333-3672590
• In person: Visit either of our branches and ask for the manager

Please share your order number so we can look up the details quickly.

Can my order be cancelled?

+

You can cancel your order before we start preparing it (usually within 5 minutes of placing). After that, since the food has been cooked, cancellation is not possible.

To cancel, WhatsApp us immediately at 0333-3672590.

Javed Nihari reserves the right to cancel any order (for example, if the item is sold out or delivery is not possible to your area). In such cases we will call you immediately.

Can prices or menu items change?

+

Yes, Javed Nihari may change menu items, prices, or availability without prior notice. The prices shown on our website at the time you place your order are the prices that apply to that order.

Do you do catering or bulk orders?

+

Yes, we cater for weddings, corporate events, and family gatherings. For bulk orders (10+ people), please WhatsApp us at 0333-3672590 at least 24 hours in advance with the details.

We also offer our signature Nihari and Haleem in tin pack form — perfect for travel, gifting, or sending to family abroad.
